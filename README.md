### Core Principles

1. **Margin of Safety** — Buy below intrinsic value
2. **Intrinsic Value** — Analyze true business value, not price
3. **Mr. Market** — Short-term market is emotion-driven
4. **Long-term Holding** — Invest for permanent holding

### Usage

After loading this skill, you can ask:
- "Analyze my investment using Graham's framework"
- "Does this stock have a margin of safety?"
- "Is XX industry worth investing in?"

### About Benjamin Graham

Benjamin Graham (1894-1976) was an English-American investor and economist, widely known as the **"Father of Value Investing."** His books *Security Analysis* (1934) and *The Intelligent Investor* (1949) are foundational texts in investment philosophy.

His most famous student was Warren Buffett, who further developed and propagated Graham's principles.

### 核心原则

1. **安全边际** — 用远低于内在价值的价格买入
2. **内在价值** — 分析企业真实价值，而非价格波动
3. **市场先生** — 短期市场是情绪驱动的投票机
4. **长期持有** — 投资是为了永久持有，不是为了卖掉

### 关于本杰明·格雷厄姆

本杰明·格雷厄姆（1894-1976），英裔美国投资家、经济学家，被广泛称为**"价值投资之父"**。他的著作《证券分析》(1934)和《聪明的投资者》(1949)是投资哲学的奠基之作。

他最著名的学生是沃伦·巴菲特，巴菲特进一步发展和传播了格雷厄姆的投资原则。
